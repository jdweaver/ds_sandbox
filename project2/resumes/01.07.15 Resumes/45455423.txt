Lesson Plan

I. Introduction (2 minutes)
* Briefly introduce myself
* Briefly introduce topic
II. Read article aloud (4 minutes)
* Have student volunteers read article "Disneyland Paris is being investigated for allegedly charging British and German visitors more than the French" aloud to class
III. Group discussion (4 minutes)
* Have students discuss questions related to the article in groups or with a partner
IV. Class discussion (5 minutes)
* Review discussion questions as a class
